About the Project

College: the notorious time of last-minute studying and endless procrastination. Students often find themselves pressed for time, fighting against deadlines and a lack of sleep.

Image credit: The Chicago Tribune

Here an elegant solution presents itself in the form of Utopia: making planning ahead enjoyable. By using a game with built-in agenda features, students gain the opportunity to become both more organized and less stressed.

Without the need to worry about remembering to meet project due dates alone, Utopia will provide an ideal support system for every college student with a hectic schedule.

Image credit: Gracepoint

However, the application does not end at students: professionals can also benefit from the app's reminder feature, as well as organizing important events (like those important meetings for work or family), becoming more organized and less stressed as a result.
